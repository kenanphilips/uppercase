who we are and what we do.

Uppercase is an integrated software provider that offers a range of solutions to clients seeking web development, design, and visual branding. At Uppercase, we listen, advise, and guide our clients through the creation process to ensure that virtual identity.

web development

From websites and web pages to electronic applications, we develop functional software to create efficient user interfaces. Our Web and Content Management System ensures that products are easily scalable, content is easily editable, and more to provide our clients and their users with unparalleled efficiency.

web design

To match our strong backend skills, we establish the right aesthetic for each website or application by determining the layout, colors, text styles, structure, images, graphics, and the use of interactive features that will best deliver pages to users. Our responsive designs build multifaceted products that are compatible with electronic devices varying in size.

branding design

Imagery is an important feature of today’s increasingly visual world. Our designers possess the artistic abilities and strategic knowledge required to create iconic logos responsible for building a solid brand identity. We also provide graphic design for print and social media to help tell a brand’s story across multiple platforms.
